I love GIS

Geographic information system is a comprehensive subject, combining geography and cartography, remote sensing and computer science, and has been widely used in different fields. It is a computer system used to input, store, query, analyze and display geographic data. GIS is a computer-based tool that can analyze and process spatial information. GIS technology integrates the unique visual effects of maps and geographic analysis functions with general database operations.
